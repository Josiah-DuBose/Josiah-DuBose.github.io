Bike MD

Bike MD is a community for motorcycle technicians and owners, a place where they can connect with each other and ask technical questions, browse common problems and solutions by model, and help each other troubleshoot vehicles more efficiently. This was my final project for the Iron Yard. I worked with two UI/UX designers to build the MVP over a two week period. Since that time I have made the project open source and we're currently in the process of redesigning the entire application. The new API will be Node/Express and the Client will in Angular 7 with RXJS.

Stack:Node, Express, MongoDB, Angular, RXJS
