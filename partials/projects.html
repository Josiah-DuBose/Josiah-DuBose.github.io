Bike MD

Bike MD is a community of motorcycle technicians and owners, a place where they can connect with each other and ask technical questions, browse common problems and solutions by model, and help each other troubleshoot vehicles more efficiently. This was my final project for the Iron Yard. I worked with two UI/UX designers to build the MVP over a two week period.

Stack: Django Rest Framework, PostgresSQL, Ember, HTML, Bootstrap, Handlebars, and Sass.

Movie Rater

This was one of my first full stack assignments at TIY. It is a light weight, movie rating app. Users can browse lists of top rated movies, view other user's watch lists, or sign up for an account and add their own ratings.


Stack: Django Rest Framework, JavaScript, HTML, Bootstrap, PostgresSQL, and CSS.
