>Projects

Bike MD

Bike MD is a community of motorcycle technicians and owners, a place where they can connect with each other and ask technical questions, browse common problems and solutions by model, and help each other troubleshoot vehicles more efficiently. This was my final project for the Iron Yard. It was originally built by myself and two UI/UX developers over a two week period. Since then I have decoupled the front and back end. A Django Rest API with serve as the backend and I am rebuilding the front end in a completely decoupled ember app.

Technologies used: Django, Rest Framework, PostgresSQL JavaScript, Ember, HTML, Handlebars, and Sass.

Dead Story

Built during a short Hackithon with UI/UX students from TIY Raleigh. The goal was to learn how to integrate with front-end developers. The assignment was to create a simple, choose your own adventure game. Ours has a zombie theme and the goal is to get to the bar and have a drink! This story is also hosted on Heroku.

Technologies used: Django Rest Framework, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, and Handlebars.


Movie Rater

This was one of my first full stack assignments at TIY. It is a light weight, movie rating app. Users can browse lists of top rated movies, browse other user's watched lists, or sign up for an account and add their own ratings. Recently I've started to revap some of the code and add more funcitonality. I'll be putting it up on Heroku soon, and adding more exciting features. Until then you can view the code on github, or clone the repo and spin up a local version on your machine.

Technologies used: Django, Rest Framework, JavaScript, HTML, PostgresSQL, and CSS.
