>Projects

Bike MD

Bike MD, hosted on Heroku, is a community of motorcycle technicians, a place where they can connect with each other and ask technical questions, browse common problems and solutions by model, and help each other troubleshoot vehicles more efficiently. This was my final project for the Iron Yard. It was built by myself and two UI/UX developers over a two week period. I was the team lead, concept creator, and solo backend/JavaScript engineer.

Dead Story

Built during a short Hackithon with UI/UX students from TIY Raleigh. The assignment was to create a short, choose your own adventure game. Ours has a zombie theme and the goal is to get to the bar and have a drink! Django Rest Framework, PostgreSQL, JavaScript and Handlebars. This story is also hosted on Heroku.

Movie Rater

This was one of my first full stack assignments at TIY. It is a light weight, movie rating app. Users can browse lists of top rated movies, browse other user's watched lists, or sign up for an account and add their own ratings. Recently I've started to revap some of the code and add more funcitonality. I'll be putting it up on Heroku soon, and adding more exciting features. Until then you can view the code on github, or clone the repo and spin up a local version on your machine.
